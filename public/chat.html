Import Presentation from Google Docs:

Please note that to view presentation from google docs you should have your presentation uploaded to Google Docs and have it published.

If not, follow the steps below or GoogleDocs help for how to publish a document to web in Google Docs.

1. Open your presentation in Google Docs

2. Click on file in the menu bar.

3. Click on publish to web

4. Click on publish and copy the link

5. The link will look similar to this

https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1KoABr8C0BB-r8T50pz6cUfL55Uk_8HPzZVryQjD4tXo/pub?start=false&loop=false&delayms=3000

6. After clicking Start the presentation will be loaded and broadcasted to all people in the room. Slide change is manual for everyone as of now.

Import Presentation from Slideshare:

Please note that to view presentation from slideshare you should have your presentation uploaded to slideshare with either as public or private.

1. If your presentation is public. The link will look similar to this :

http://www.slideshare.net/_your_user_name_/_your_slide_name

Example:

http://www.slideshare.net/hculver/10-ways-to-know-if-your-speech-sucks

2. If your presentation is private. The secret link to share with people will look similar to this :

http://www.slideshare.net/secret/_your_secret_url

Example:

http://www.slideshare.net/secret/3p9LCdkhmQrTHV

To check if your slide was uploaded private or public or in case of private presentation, if you have not generated a secret url for sharing with people. Follow these steps.

1. Login to your Slideshare account.

2. Open your already uploaded presentation in slideshare either by clicking My Uploads or otherwise.

3. Find and click the Edit option for the particular presentation and go to privacy settings tab.

4. If presentation is marked public simply copy the link from the browser address bar and enter it in the public link section and proceed.

If presentation is marked private:

5. In option Who can view(on slideshare) change Only Me to People with the secret link

6. In option Allow Embeds choose Anywhere.

7. Click on Update to update the privacy settings. Now copy the secret link from option Who can view

The link will look similar to this:

http://www.slideshare.net/secret/3p9LCdkhmQrTHV
