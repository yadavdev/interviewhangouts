Import Presentation from googledocs:

Please note that to view presentation from google docs you should have your presentation uploaded to googledocs and have it published.

If not follow the steps below or GoogleDocs help for how to publish document from google docs.

1. Open your presentation in googledocs

2. Click on file in the menu bar.

3. Click on publish to web

4. Click on publish and copy the link

5. The link will look similar to this

https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1KoABr8C0BB-r8T50pz6cUfL55Uk_8HPzZVryQjD4tXo/pub?start=false&loop=false&delayms=3000

6. After clicking Start the presentation will be loaded and broadcasted to all people in the room. And slides for others will automatically change once you change it.
